About the Event

Empower the Future is a groundbreaking 24-hour event where participants will learn to build AI-driven businesses from scratch. This intensive program brings together students, young professionals, and tech enthusiasts to:

  • Learn from industry experts about AI and business development
  • Form teams and develop AI-powered business concepts
  • Receive mentorship from successful entrepreneurs
  • Pitch to a panel of investors and industry leaders

As a sponsor, your brand will be at the center of this innovation hub, connecting with the next generation of tech leaders.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of companies typically sponsor this event? +

We welcome sponsors from across the tech ecosystem, including AI startups, established tech companies, venture capital firms, educational institutions, and service providers targeting the tech industry (legal, accounting, cloud services, etc.).

Can we provide our own branded materials for the event? +

Absolutely! We encourage sponsors to provide banners, swag, and other branded materials. We'll work with you to determine optimal placement based on your sponsorship level.

How will our sponsorship be recognized digitally? +

Depending on your sponsorship level, your logo and link will appear on our event website, email newsletters to 10,000+ subscribers, social media posts (50k+ followers across platforms), and in our mobile app used by all attendees.

Can we participate in the event beyond sponsorship? +

Yes! Many sponsors choose to have representatives serve as mentors, judges, or workshop leaders. This provides even deeper engagement with participants.

What is the deadline for sponsorship commitments? +

We accept sponsorships on a rolling basis, but prime placement opportunities are allocated first-come, first-served. We recommend committing at least 6 weeks before the event for maximum visibility.
